Supplier Diversity Registration Form

We would like to thank you for visiting the Nippon Seiki International Supplier Diversity Registration Portal. Our company, Nippon Seiki International, recognizes the importance of connecting qualified diverse businesses with sourcing opportunities to drive economic development and growth in our communities.

Creating a dynamic and diverse supply chain is an important goal for Nippon Seiki International. As part of our strategic sourcing efforts, we aim to include diverse suppliers where alignment exists between our business needs and the needs of our customers.
