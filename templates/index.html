Supplier Diversity Registration Form 5

Welcome to the ABC Corporation Supplier Diversity Registration Site. At ABC Corporation, we recognize that connecting qualified diverse businesses with sourcing opportunities provides both economic development and growth in our communities, as well as enables our customers to achieve their supplier diversity goals.

ABC Corporation is committed to creating a diverse and dynamic supply chain. Our goal is to include diverse suppliers in our sourcing efforts where there is alignment with our business needs and the needs of our customers.

What is a Diverse Supplier?

For the purpose of this program, a supplier is considered diverse if they are a U.S. privately held company that is 51% owned and operated by an individual who falls into one or more of the following categories:

Women
Hispanic American
African American
Asian American
Native American/ Alaskan Native
Veteran
Disabled Veteran
Hubzone
Small Disadvantaged Business
Please note: this site is for certified diverse companies only. All non-diverse supplier submissions will be deleted.
